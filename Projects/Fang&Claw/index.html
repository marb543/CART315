How to Play

  • ⬅️ ➡️ Arrow Keys: Move claw left/right
  • ⬆️ ⬇️ Arrow Keys: Move claw up/down
  • Space: Open/Close claw to grab bats
  • Warning: Bats might fly away at the top!

Power-ups:

  • Magnetic Claw - Larger grab range
  • Double Points - Score twice the points
  • Slow Motion - Slows down all bats
